23. $\{x, x+2, x+4, x+6, x+8\}$ and $\{x+1, x+3, x+5, x+7, x+9\}$ are two groups of numbers. Which of the following is/are true?

\begin{itemize}
    \item[I.] The two groups of numbers have the same range.
    \item[II.] The two groups of numbers have the same standard deviation.
    \item[III.] The two groups of numbers have the same mean.
\end{itemize}

\begin{itemize}
    \item[A.] I only
    \item[B.] II only
    \item[C.] III only
    \item[D.] I and II only
    \item[E.] I and III only
\end{itemize}

    \item[II.] $\Delta ABC \sim \Delta EAC$
    \item[III.] $EAC$ is an isosceles triangle

\begin{itemize}
    \item[A.] I only
    \item[B.] III only
    \item[C.] I and II only
    \item[D.] I and III only
    \item[E.] I, II and III
\end{itemize}
