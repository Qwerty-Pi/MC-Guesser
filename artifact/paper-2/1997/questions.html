FORMULAS FOR REFERENCE

SPHERE Surface area = \(4\pi r^{2}\) Volume = \(\dfrac{4}{3}\pi r^{3}\)
CYLINDER Area of curved surface = \(2\pi rh\) Volume = \(\pi r^{2}h\)
CONE Area of curved surface = \(\pi rl\) Volume = \(\dfrac{1}{3}\pi r^{2}h\)
PRISM Volume = base area \(\times\) height
PYRAMID Volume = \(\dfrac{1}{3} \times\) base area \(\times\) height
